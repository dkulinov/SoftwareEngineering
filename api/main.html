About:

We are a global group of energy and petrochemical companies with an average of 86,000 employees in more than 70 countries. We use advanced technologies and take an innovative approach to help build a sustainable energy future. Oil & Gas Co is an international energy company that aims to meet the world’s growing need for more and cleaner energy solutions in ways that are economically, environmentally and socially responsible.

Values:

At Oil & Gas Co, we share a set of core values – honesty, integrity and respect for people – which underpin all the work we do. The Oil & Gas Co General Business Principles, Code of Conduct and Ethics and Compliance Manual help everyone at Oil & Gas Co act in line with these values and comply with relevant laws and regulations.
